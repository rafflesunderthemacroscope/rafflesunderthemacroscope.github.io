Under The Macroscope

An initiative by RECAS to keep you updated with the latest news.

Presenting to you the week's latest news in interesting bite-sized bits.

No time to read lengthy news articles during the week? Have no fear, RECAS is here! Our weekly magazine, Under the Macroscope is dedicated to bringing you the latest news in a concise, fun and interesting manner. By joining our mailing list, you will receive a weekly email bringing to you "what's happened this week". By liking our Facebook page, you will receive the latest updates on your newsfeed as well, giving you convenient access to the latest news as you log on to Facebook. Whether to keep up with the latest news, to expand your general knowledge or to prepare for General Paper, UtM has something for you. Subscribe to us today!
